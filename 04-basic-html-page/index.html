How to Make A Sandwich

    Step 1: Toast 2 pieces of bread for 3 minutes
    Step 2: Lay 2 pieces of bread on plate
    Step 3: Take out 2 pieces of cheese and lay on top of bread
    Step 4: Cook scrambled eggs and lay on top of cheese when fully cooked so the cheese melts
    Step 5: Get 2 pieces of turkey and lay on top of the eggs to consolidate the sandwich
    Step 6: Flip 1 piece of bread on top of the other. Be careful not to spill the insides
    Step 7: ENJOY!
